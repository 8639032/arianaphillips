About Me




My name is Ariana Phillips, and I am 17 years old. I'm a native of Charlotte, but I will never claim a single sports team hailing from my city. In fact, I'll never claim any sports team, but that's beyond the point. In my free time, I'm an avid writer, and I enjoy engaging in volunteer work. A few years ago, I regularly published short stories online, amassing over 1.5 million reads. While my responsibilities interrupted my writing, I still volunteer whenever I can. Recently, I volunteered for small businesses at Charlotte's newly opened VAPA Center. As a treat for reaching the end of my About Me paragraph, I'm going to give you a fun fact! I've graced 5 U.S. states, and I dream of visiting all of them.



Bonus Fun Facts!

  • I'm the eldest daughter, but I try not to make the fact my entire identity.

  • Throughout my academic career, I've earned over 35 awards and certificates.

  • I'm nearly legally blind due to astigmatism. I've been told that the condition is something I shouldn't be proud of, but I was born this way, and I refuse to be vision-shamed.
